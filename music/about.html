What is tunix?

Tunix is a free-to-use music service that let you discover and listen to not only known music from your favorite artist, but also lost treassures of live events and un-published material. This is all made possible by the extensive YouTube library of music, the fuel of Tunix. There are millions of tracks available on Tunix. Make your own playlists and enjoy the exclusive music.
